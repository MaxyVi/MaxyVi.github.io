BIO

Who am I

Hi there, my name is Max Vali and I am a major in Information Science, with a minor in Creative Technology Design. I am a Colorado Native, growing up right outside of Boulder in Louisville. I consider myself a bit of a geek as I have always been facinated by computers, and learning more about basic functions in our day to day life. I hope to help an environmental based company in the future, with either data analysis or product design.

Hobbies

Living in Colorado I have numerous hobbies that I enjoy often. With many great ski resorts nearby, I am able to go on weekly skiing trips with family and friends. Whenever there is not snow, it means there are trials to mountain bike! When weather in uncooperative like usual in Colorado, I often kick back and do a puzzel or watch sports.

Academics

I am currently a senior at the University of Colorado in Boulder. I have been taking computational classes in programming and graphic design. Over the last couple of years I have been able to gain expiernece with many powerful softwares. Ranging from Adobe programs, to Tableau I love making graphics driven by data. I find that well laid out figures can evoke more emotion than words.
